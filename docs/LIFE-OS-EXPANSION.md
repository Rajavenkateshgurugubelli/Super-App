# 🚀 Expansion: The "Life Operating System" (Epics 9-13)

> **Status:** ADOPTED (Roadmap)  
> **Strategic Objective:** Resolve high-friction daily problems in Global Markets (US, EU, India).

---

## 🏛️ New High-Value Modules

### 1. Epic 9: Wealth & Tax Optimization (High Priority)
A global financial intelligence layer for cross-border professionals.
- **Key Feature**: Unified tracking of 401(k) [US], ISAs [UK], and NRO/NRE [India] accounts.
- **Mechanism**: AI-driven tax harvesting and DTAA (Double Tax Avoidance Agreement) compliance.
- **Integration**: Influences the **Unified Wallet** currency choice for high-value transactions.

### 2. Epic 10: G2C (Government-to-Citizen) Vault (High Priority)
A lifecycle manager for global identity and residency documents.
- **Key Feature**: Proactive alerts for Passport/Visa expiry and Tax Filing deadlines.
- **Mechanism**: Secure, encrypted "Vault" with OIDC-based pre-verification for health/finance modules.
- **Integration**: Powers high-assurance identity flows in the **IAM** system.

### 3. Epic 11: Auto-Guardian (Medium Priority)
A proactive physical asset manager for vehicle owners.
- **Key Feature**: Digital service history and AI-driven DIY repair guides.
- **Mechanism**: Automated "Service Kit" ordering via the **Logistics** and **Delivery** modules.
- **Integration**: Real-time vehicle safety data linked to the **Health** and **InsurTech** nodes.

### 4. Epic 12: Pet-Guardian (Medium Priority)
Specialized veterinary and behavioral tracking node.
- **Key Feature**: Telemedicine for pets and "Safety Scanners" for toxic household items.
- **Mechanism**: Automated subscription for high-quality specialized feed via **Logistics**.
- **Integration**: Leverages the **Health** module's scheduling and **Social** engagement circles.

### 5. Epic 13: Professional Growth & Research Node (Low Priority)
An academic and career assistant for high-level researchers and professionals.
- **Key Feature**: AI-driven reference management and PhD application tracker.
- **Mechanism**: Professional "Networking Circles" in the **Social** engine.
- **Integration**: Academic collaboration layer built on top of the **Social** messaging engine.
